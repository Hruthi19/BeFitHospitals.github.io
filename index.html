About Us


We take care of your healthy life

Our team is committed to providing the highest quality treatments and the most empathetic care to the community that we serve. Situated on the third floor of The Tornado Tower, Be Fit Hospitals encompasses 22 clinics covering most major specialties, in addition to diagnostic imaging and laboratory services, physical rehabilitation, family medicine, and a day-case surgical unit. We offer fast and uncomplicated access to our doctors, accurate diagnoses, and highly effective treatments. We believe that technology and medicine go hand in hand, which is why we provide the latest in medical technology to our patients.

Meniscal Tear

A meniscus tear is an injury to a part of your knee called the meniscus and is a common injury. The menisci are two crescent-shaped pads of thick, rubbery shock-absorbing cartilage in your knee joint.
